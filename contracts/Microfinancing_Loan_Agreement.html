6. INTEREST

The Loan shall carry interest at the rate specified in the Sanction Letter and the KFS, determined in accordance with the Lender’s prevailing Interest Rate Policy and in compliance with Applicable Laws.

Unless otherwise specified in the Sanction Letter, interest shall accrue from the Disbursement Date on the outstanding principal balance of the Loan and shall be calculated on a reducing balance basis, using a 365-day year for actual days elapsed.

Interest shall be debited to the Loan account at such intervals as may be specified in the Sanction Letter and shall form part of the Borrower’s repayment obligations under the Repayment Schedule attached as Schedule-4 to this Agreement.

Any change in the Interest Rate shall be made only in accordance with the Lender’s Interest Policy and Applicable Laws, and shall be communicated to the Borrower in writing or in such manner as may be agreed between the Borrower and the Lender.

If the Borrower fails to pay any interest on the due date, the Lender may, without prejudice to any of its rights, capitalise such unpaid interest (to the extent permitted under Applicable Laws), which shall thereafter carry interest at the rate applicable to the Loan.

If the Borrower defaults in payment of any instalment or part thereof on the due date, the Borrower shall be liable to pay Overdue Interest or penal charges, calculated only on the overdue amount, at the rate specified in the Sanction Letter and the KFS, from the date of default until the date of actual payment.

7. REPAYMENT

The Borrower shall repay the Loan, together with all interest, charges, and other amounts payable under this Agreement, in the instalments and on the due dates specified in the Repayment Schedule. The Repayment Schedule shall form an integral part of this Agreement.

All repayments shall be made by the Borrower by bank transfer to the Lender’s account or any other permissible mode, in immediately available and freely transferable funds, without any set-off or counterclaim. The Borrower shall ensure that payments are received by the Lender on or before the relevant due dates.

Unless otherwise agreed, payments received from the Borrower shall be appropriated in the following order:

  1. costs, expenses, and other charges (if any);
  2. Overdue Interest or penal charges;
  3. accrued interest; and
  4. principal outstanding.

The Borrower may, at any time during the tenure of the Loan, prepay the whole or any part of the outstanding principal amount, together with interest accrued thereon, without any prepayment penalty. Upon such prepayment, the Repayment Schedule shall be revised accordingly and communicated to the Borrower.

Any payment made by the Borrower prior to the relevant due date shall first be appropriated towards amounts already due and payable, and any surplus may, at the Lender’s discretion, be applied towards future instalments, in which case the Lender may revise the Repayment Schedule.

If the Borrower fails to pay any instalment in full on its due date:

  1. the Borrower shall be liable to pay Overdue Interest or penal charges calculated only on the overdue amount, from the date of default until payment; and
  2. such default shall constitute an Event of Default under this Agreement.

Where the Borrower makes a partial prepayment, the Lender may, in consultation with the Borrower, either reduce the amount of future instalments or shorten the remaining loan tenure, as per the Borrower’s preference and in accordance with the Lender’s policy.

8. EVENTS OF DEFAULT

Each of the following shall constitute an “Event of Default” under this Agreement:

  1. Failure by the Borrower to pay any instalment of principal, interest, Overdue Interest, or any other amount payable under this Agreement on its due date and such failure continues for a period of 7 (seven) days.
  2. The Borrower utilises the Loan or any part thereof for Purposes other than those stated in the Loan Application and/or specified in the Sanction Letter, including for any unlawful, speculative, or prohibited activities under Applicable Laws.
  3. Failure by the Borrower to observe or perform any term, covenant, condition, representation, or warranty contained in this Agreement, the Sanction Letter, or the KFS, and such failure is not cured within 15 (fifteen) days after written notice from the Lender.
  4. Any representation, warranty, statement, or information given by the Borrower in connection with the Loan Application, the Sanction Letter, this Agreement, or the KFS is found to be incorrect, misleading, or materially incomplete when made or deemed to have been made.
  5. A material adverse change has occurred in the Borrower’s financial condition, livelihood, or ability to meet repayment obligations.
  6. The Borrower defaults in payment of any indebtedness to any other lender or financial institution, or commits a breach of the terms of any other loan agreement, which in the opinion of the Lender may affect the Borrower’s repayment capacity under this Loan.
  7. The Borrower becomes insolvent, bankrupt, or is unable to pay debts as they fall due.
  8. In case of the Borrower’s death, where the Outstanding Amounts are not settled by the Borrower’s legal heirs or estate within 60 (sixty) days of such occurrence.
  9. The Borrower changes residence, place of business, employment, or contact details without promptly notifying the Lender, and remains untraceable for a continuous period of 30 (thirty) days.

Upon the occurrence of any Event of Default, the Lender may, without prejudice to any other rights or remedies available under Applicable Laws:

  1. declare the entire Outstanding Amounts under the Loan, including accrued interest and charges, to be immediately due and payable;
  2. suspend further disbursement (if any portion remains undisbursed);
  3. recover the outstanding dues in accordance with its Fair Practices Code and RBI’s guidelines on recovery;
  4. report the default to Credit Information Companies as per Applicable Laws; and/or
  5. require the Borrower to indemnify and keep indemnified the Lender in accordance with the Indemnity Clause of this Agreement.
